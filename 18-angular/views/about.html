About Angular Caffe

Angular Caffe is a small demo café — a learning project built to explore the core ideas of AngularJS: two-way binding, filters, ng-repeat, routing and template includes. The menu you browse is real data living in a controller scope, sorted and filtered entirely on the client.

What’s under the hood

  • Routing ngRoute swaps views into ng-view without a page reload.
  • Binding Search, sort and rating filters update the table live as you type.
  • Includes The header and the add-dish form are pulled in with ng-include.
